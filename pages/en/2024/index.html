About We Love Speed

Created in 2018 by and for the webperf community, the "{{ site.title }}" not-for-profit conference is born from the desire to share as widely as possible knowledge and experience about webperf. Web professionals, e-commerce and webperf experts, this event is for you!

We expect up to 250 participants, including executives, developers, enthusiasts and experts in web performance, as every year. But in 2024, we want to contribute even more to the democratization of web performance.

Therefore, for this edition, we aim at a more heterogeneous audience, including profiles such as Chief Digital Officer, e-commerce, marketing or SEO. We will select conferences adapted to all sensibilities. There will be something for everyone!
